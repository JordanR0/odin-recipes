Ratatouille

Description

A vegetable stew of Provence, typically consisting of eggplant,zucchini, onions, green peppers, tomatoes, and garlic, served hot or cold.

Ingredients

  • eggplant
  • zucchini
  • tomatoes
  • sauce of crushed tomatoes
  • herbs
  • garlic
  • onion
  • 1 Red & green bell pepper
  • Salt
  • Handful of pine nuts
  • 250 grams of brown mushroom

Steps

  1. Roast Nuts
  2. Prepare Eggplant
  3. Slice the Onion
  4. Cook the Onion
  5. Add Tomato Puree
  6. Cook Peppers
  7. Mushrooms and Zucchini
  8. Eggplant
  9. Add Tomatoes
  10. Add Rest of Vegetables and Season
