Peanutbutter and Jelly

Description

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich consists of peanut butter and fruit preserves—jelly—spread on bread. The sandwich may be open-faced, made of a single slice of bread folded over, or made using two slices of bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of bread
  • jelly
  • Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Get 2 Slices of bread
  2. On one slice of beard spread peanut butter on it evenly
  3. On the other slice of beard spread jelly on it evenly
  4. Combine both pieces of beard
